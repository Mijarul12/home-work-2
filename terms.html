Terms and Conditions for Home Work

Welcome to Home Work. These Terms and Conditions ("Terms") govern your use of our website and services. By registering an account and using our platform, you agree to be bound by these Terms. Please read them carefully.

1. Registration and Accounts

  • User (Worker) Registration: To work on projects, you must register as a "User" and pay a one-time, non-refundable verification fee of ₹499. This fee is for administrative purposes and to ensure the seriousness of applicants.
  • Student (Client) Registration: Students can register for free but must fund their wallet to post a project.
  • Account Security: You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account password. Home Work is not liable for any loss resulting from unauthorized account access.

2. Payments, Fees, and Withdrawals

  • Project Funding: Students must deposit funds into their wallet before a project can be started. These funds are held in escrow.
  • Payment Release: Funds are released to the User only after the Student reviews and approves the delivered work.
  • Withdrawal Threshold: Users can request a withdrawal of their earnings once their wallet balance reaches a minimum of ₹1499.
  • Withdrawal Processing: All withdrawals are processed within 7 working days (excluding bank holidays and weekends).

3. Project Deadlines and Delivery

  • Commitment: By claiming a project "slot," a User commits to delivering the work by the specified deadline.
  • Penalties for Lateness: Failure to meet a deadline without prior communication with the Admin may result in a penalty, lower rating, or account suspension.
  • "Ghosting": Accepting a project and failing to deliver it (ghosting) will result in an immediate and permanent ban, with forfeiture of any wallet balance.

4. Quality of Work and Plagiarism

  • Originality: All work submitted by Users must be 100% original. Plagiarism is strictly forbidden and will lead to account termination.
  • AI-Generated Content: The use of AI tools to generate entire responses is prohibited. AI may be used for brainstorming, but the final work must be human-authored.
  • Revisions: Students may request reasonable revisions if the delivered work does not meet the initial project requirements.

5. Prohibited Activities

  • Off-Platform Communication: Users and Students are strictly forbidden from sharing personal contact information (email, phone number, etc.) to transact outside of Home Work.
  • Illegal or Unethical Requests: The platform may not be used for any illegal activities, including but not limited to academic dishonesty, hate speech, or creating malicious software.
  • Refund Abuse: Students found to be falsely claiming poor quality to obtain a refund while using the work will be banned.

6. Dispute Resolution

  • If a Student is unsatisfied with the work, they can raise a dispute.
  • A Home Work Admin will mediate the dispute by reviewing the project requirements and the delivered work.
  • The Admin's decision on payment release or refund is final.

7. Termination

Home Work reserves the right to suspend or terminate any account that violates these Terms. The non-refundable ₹499 registration fee will not be returned in cases of termination due to policy violations.

8. Changes to Terms

We may update these Terms from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes, and your continued use of the platform will constitute acceptance of the new Terms.
